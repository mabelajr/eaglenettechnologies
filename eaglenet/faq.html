Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

What services does Eaglenet provide?

Eaglenet provides high-speed internet services with secure connections and reliable uptime for both residential and business customers.

How do I sign up for Eaglenet services?

You can sign up through our website by clicking on the "Sign Up" link in the navigation menu, or by calling our customer service.

Technical Questions

What should I do if my connection is slow?

First, try restarting your modem and router. If the problem persists, contact our technical support team.

How do I reset my password?

Go to the login page and click on "Forgot Password." You'll receive instructions via email to reset your password.

Billing Questions

When is my payment due?

Payments are due on the 1st of each month. You can check your exact due date in your account dashboard.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept credit cards, debit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers.
